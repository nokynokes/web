Jack and the Beanstalk

Once Upon a Time...

....there lived a poor widow who had an only son named Jack. She was very poor, for times had been hard, and Jack was too young to work.

Almost all the furniture of the little cottage had been sold to buy bread, until at last there was nothing left worth selling...

Only the good cow,Milky White, remained, and she gave milk every morning, which they took to market and sold. But one sad day Milky White gave no milk, and then things looked bad indeed.
